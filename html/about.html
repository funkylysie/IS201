About Me

This is what I do.

Hi! I'm Allyse Ballard Funk. I recently got married to Tanner Funk this past February (pictured above in the top right), and we're loving life! Other than that, I decided that even though I have enjoyed IS 201 so much this past semester, I would like to apply to the Marketing program at BYU. My husband is currently in the IS program, and together, we are building our own business! "Better Dating" is our subscription business of pre-planned dates. We wanted to provide a service for people where they can get fun date ideas with live coupons.

One of the big things I value in life is family. My family (pictured above in the top left) has been a huge example and support for me during the ups and downs of life. When I met my husband's family (pictured in the bottom right; mom, younger sister, and brother missing from the photo), I could tell that they had similar values and additional great ones.

I spent my whole life growing up living in Las Vegas, Nevada, but I love to travel and see new places. I also love trying new things. For example, this past summer, I picked up learning how to slalom (pictured in the bottom left) and... you could say that I'm still learning. I also started learning how to play tennis hence my tennis background at the top. One of my favorite activities is to play tennis with my husband. We are very excited for the warm weather.

Another big part of my life is my faith. I served a mission in the Philippines Cebu East Mission and it changed my life. Since being back, I've worked at the MTC as a teacher and supervisor. Working there is one of the highlights of my day.
